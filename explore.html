Welcome to Task Management Application

1. You can create tasks by clicking on "Create Task" as provided.

2. You will be providing the name of the person to whom you are assigning the task.

3. Also, provide the description of that task, the start date and the end date.

4. Click 'Submit' for designating the task.

5. For editing or deleting the task, click on 'Manage Task' button.

6. To change the status of the given task, click on 'Update Task'.

7. The 'Logout' button will redirect you to the Login page.
